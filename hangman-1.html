INSTRUCTIONS TO PLAY THE HANGMAN

Step-1: Open Hangman Application

Step-2: Choose the letters according to your choice in the limited number of attempts

Step-3: Once the word is completed successfully, the word will be displayed and a message will come - “You Won”.

Step-4: If the word is not completed in the limited no. of guesses the picture of hangman is completed.

Step-5: When the word is not attempted correctly then after step-4, you will have an option of RESET the game which will start again with the new word and your 1 point will be deducted from your score.

Step-6: If the user is not able to guess the word, he/she can click on the SOLVE button, it will be more of like a help/hint button for them.

Step-7: When the user wants to play a new game again, he/she can click on the PLAY button to start a new game.

Points sytem : 10 points for every correct guess.
